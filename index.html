Intro

I have made it a priority to continuously mentor and provide assistance to those in my community as I believe information is power. Following my participation in the Yale Young African Scholars program in 2023, I took on a role as a Content Writer at a student-led startup which has raised the awareness of over a thousand international students concerning the college application process, gaining global traction with usage in India. I also expanded my knowledge through prerequisite courses offered by the Massachusetts Institute of Technology’s Beaver Works Institute: courses in Microelectronics and Quantum Software. By the way, check out my awesome work.

In Chrisland High School Ikeja, I served in various leadership roles from 2018 to 2024. As the Head Boy of the school, I brought about increased student morale with general school spirit raising by 30% according to surveys. As the Captain of the Science Team, I led the team to multiple victories, including the National Olympiad where I placed among the 40 best Physics candidates in Nigeria.

Furthermore, I was the Chief Student Editor of the yearbook, a role in which I led a team of 6 students who oversaw the publication of the school’s yearbook. I was also the captain of the school debate team, where I served as a mentor to the younger students and led them to national success in the Association of International School Educators in Nigeria Debate Competition.

Currently, I'm pursuing a gap year in which I am exploring various skills and research opportunities, from intensifying my learning of the German language to tutorials and projects relating to machine learning and neural networks.
